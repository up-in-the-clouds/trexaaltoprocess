Consumers

This section is for you, the consumer of clothing and household textile products.

Here you can find practical tools and information to support you in making more conscious choices when purchasing, using, or disposing of textile items.

waste Hierarchy

In this section, the textile waste hierarchy will be explained. We will also go into detail on what the hierarchy of consumption in the circular economy is and how it can help guide your textile consumption and disposal practices.

circular consumption tool

We built a tool for you that gives you different options of what to do if you want to get a new clothing item or do not use a clothing item anymore – all in line with circular consumption practices. We listed examples of repair instructions, donation options, or rental companies in eight different countries, so you can find the one that’s the most fitting for you.

for textile waste disposal

Here you find a list of different places to dispose of textile waste – textile items that cannot be reused anymore.
