Collecting, Sorting, Recycling

This section looks at what happens to textiles when they're no longer needed or wanted. We'll see how they are collected, get sorted, who handles them, and what can be done with them afterward. Through easy-to-understand videos, we'll learn about the steps involved in managing textile waste properly. We'll also explain why it's important to dispose of textiles responsibly and how everyone can help make a difference.
